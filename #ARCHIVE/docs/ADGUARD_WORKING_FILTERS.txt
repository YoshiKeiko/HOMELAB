HOW TO ADD IN ADGUARD:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Open AdGuard: http://localhost:8084
2. Filters → DNS blocklists
3. Click "Add blocklist" → "Add a custom list"
4. Paste ONE URL at a time
5. Click "Add"
6. Repeat for each filter
